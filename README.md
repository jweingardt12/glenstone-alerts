# Glenstone Alerts

![Glenstone Alerts](public/og.jpg)

A simple tool to get notified when free timed-entry [Glenstone Museum](https://glenstone.org) tickets open up.

## What It Does

- Monitors Glenstone reservation availability and spots newly released tickets.
- Sends you an email when tickets appear for your preferred dates and party size.
- Shows a quick weather outlook to help you pick a great day.
- Includes direct links back to Glenstone to complete your booking quickly.

## How To Use

1. Browse the calendar to see upcoming dates.
2. Select the days you care about and choose your party size (and time window if you like).
3. Enter your email and create the alert.
4. When tickets open up for your preferences, you’ll receive an email with a link to book on glenstone.org.
5. Manage alerts anytime from the link included in every email (pause, update, or delete).

## What To Expect

- Ticket availability changes quickly; alerts don’t guarantee tickets will still be there.
- The system checks availability roughly every 30 minutes.
- To avoid spam, alerts include a 24‑hour cooldown between notifications for the same alert.
- Works great on phones and desktops.

## Privacy

- Your email is used solely to send alerts and manage your alerts.
- Basic, privacy‑friendly analytics help improve the experience; no personal profiles or selling of data.
